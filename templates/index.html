About This Website

A centralized Blood Inventory Management System for keeping track of the blood stock across numerous blood banks.This website aims at maintaining all information regarding blood donors , different blood groups available in different blood banks as well as blood camps and help them manage in a better way. Through this web app anyone who is interested in donating the blood can register himself and can also make request for blood online. Similarly, if any organization wants to register itself with this site they can also register.
